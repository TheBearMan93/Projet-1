Roadtrip through the US | Close

The United States is one of the most distinct and fascinating collection of experiences you’ll ever come across. With cultures gathered from the far corners of the world, there’s no place on earth with quite the same variety of art, music, and human stories. And the vast landscape is just as diverse and awe-inspiring as the people you’ll meet.

So if you’re ready for the ultimate U.S. road trip, enjoy here my interactive map presenting 50 stops that will take you on a round-trip route accross all 48 contiguous states.

This trip allows you to stops at some of the majors National sites (parks, monuments, etc.) in each state. This path minimize the amount of time spent driving and maximize the time spent to enjoy the landmarks. The trip itselft would likely take 2-3 months.

It is, of course, possible to visit other National sites or any other place between two destinations. This map only shows the best way to discover America's history in the most efficient way.

Enjoy the ride!
